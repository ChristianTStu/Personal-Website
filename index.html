About Me

Hello! I'm a dynamic and innovative Solutions Engineer at Pricespider, a leading company in California, where I specialize in eCommerce and Data Analytics. My expertise lies in conducting product demonstrations of web-based applications, providing brands with actionable insights for consumer conversion. My toolkit includes advanced proficiency in Excel, Google Looker, and a foundational understanding of Python.

Currently, I am pursuing a Master's in Cybersecurity and Information Operations from the University of Arizona. This endeavor is more than just an academic pursuit; it's a stepping stone towards my goal of transitioning into the cybersecurity realm, with a keen interest in Security Architecture and Engineering. A highlight of my Master's program was developing a novel form of covert communication using Python, showcasing my ability to innovate and solve complex problems.

Looking ahead, I am excited about the prospect of applying my skills and knowledge in a role within the Public Sector, particularly with the Department of Defense, or pursuing a commission as a Naval Officer in roles such as Cryptological Warfare Officer or Cyber Warfare Engineer. These positions represent the perfect amalgamation of my passion for cybersecurity and my commitment to serving.

Outside of my professional life, I am an avid video game enthusiast and have a deep fascination with space and technology, especially in the realms of astronomy and physics. These interests keep me constantly curious and engaged with the latest advancements in science and technology.

Let's connect and explore how we can drive innovation and security in the digital world together!
